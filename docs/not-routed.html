Domain not routed — 404

This domain points to fortrabbit but it isn't associated with any App. Are you the developer? Maybe you have not registered the domain with the App yet? You might want to go to the Dashboard to add this domain to your App.

Other possibilities

  • recently upgraded App, but DNS cache is not - yet - refreshed
  • A records not CNAME used for routing, now the App was moved
  • misconfiguration naked domain (domain.tld) and subdomain (blog.domain.tld)
